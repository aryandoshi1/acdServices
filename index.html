Planocate

Planocate by Optimizer is an innovative Proof of Concept pitch that harnesses the power of AI to optimize personal schedules based on individual preferences. I had the privilege of taking on the role of product manager and leading this pitch during a thrilling Shark Tank Competition, where we presented the idea to a diverse audience of 150+ Rutgers alumni. To demonstrate the unique value proposition of our concept, we created a captivating Figma Mock-up Video showcasing the intuitive UI/UX design of our prototype.
